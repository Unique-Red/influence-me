Founder's Path

Sarah Temitope Iwajomo is the Founder of InfluenceMe Agency and an Influencer Marketing Consultant with over six years of experience in the film, media, and marketing industries.

She has worked on major film and entertainment projects, including Netflix and cinema releases, while leading influencer marketing campaigns for brands across FMCG, banking, skincare, food and beverage, technology, telecommunications, and consumer electronics sectors.

Sarah specializes in influencer strategy, creator partnerships, talent management, and campaign execution, helping brands drive awareness, engagement, and business growth through impactful influencer marketing. Through InfluenceMe Agency, she continues to connect brands with culture, communities, and creators to deliver measurable results.
